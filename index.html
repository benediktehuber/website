Welcome!

Welcome! My name is Benedikte Grundtvig Huber, and I am a Master’s student in Political Science at the University of Copenhagen.

I submitted my master's thesis in the summer of 2024, and I am now spending the last part of my studies as a visiting researcher at Princeton University in order to broaden my academic horizon.

My research interests lie broadly in Comparative Politics. I am mainly interested in regime development, authoritarianism, and the safeguards of democracies.

While studying, I taught Comparative Politics and worked as a research assistant at multiple Universities.

In my spare time, I enjoy reading magical realism, going to concerts, and cooking. I was born in Germany, and hold passports from Denmark and Austria. Don’t hesitate to reach out!
