Work in Progress

Anatomy of Empire (ANATEM) Database
With Jacob Gerner Hariri and Asger Mose Wingender. Building a large-scale city-year dataset to study state formation, empires, and long-run political development across time and space.

The Polish Question: Nation-Building under Imperial Rule (1772–1918)
My dissertation research examines nation-building under the Polish partitions, using genealogical records and naming practices to study identity, assimilation, and political incorporation over the long run.

From Nobility to Democratic Politics: The Persistence of the Polish Szlachta
With Marc Sabatier Hvidkjær and Krystof Krakowski. Using genealogical records and naming patterns, we study the persistence of noble identity in contemporary Poland and its relationship to political representation and local governance.
